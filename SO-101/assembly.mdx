# Assembly

Follow the instructions in the [SO-101 HuggingFace docs](https://huggingface.co/docs/lerobot/so101)
to assemble the SO-101 robot. Source the parts and setup
each of the 5 motors. Stop when you reach the "Configure the Motors" section.
